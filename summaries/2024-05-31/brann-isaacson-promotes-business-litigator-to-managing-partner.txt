Attorney Stacy Stitham will become the new managing partner of the law firm Brann & Isaacson, effective July 1. She will succeed Martin Eisenstein, who has led the firm for over 20 years. Stitham has significant experience in legal practice and firm management, specializing in intellectual property law, litigation, and online and multichannel marketing. She has held leadership roles in the community, including president of the Maine State Bar Association and board service at the Maine Justice Foundation. Stitham is also an educator, teaching business law at Thomas College, and holds degrees from Harvard College and Harvard Law School.
