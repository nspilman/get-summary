WEX Inc., a financial technology provider in Portland, has promoted Sara Trickett to the role of chief legal officer and corporate secretary, effective June 4, 2024. She succeeds Hilary Rapkin, who is retiring but will stay with the company as an executive advisor until March 2025. Trickett has been with WEX since 2021 and has held roles including deputy general counsel and vice president, corporate legal services. Prior to WEX, she worked at Visa and Latham & Watkins. WEX provides financial technology services in sectors like fleet mobility, corporate payments, and travel industry payments.
