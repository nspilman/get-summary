**Summary:**

Market Basket, a Massachusetts-based grocery store chain, is set to open its third branch in Maine. The new 80,000-square-foot supermarket will be located at 169 Topsham Fair Mall Road in Topsham and is scheduled to open on June 14. The new store will offer around 50,000 grocery items, including a butcher shop, take-out kitchen, and a cafe with seating. The store aims to create approximately 300 jobs and provide additional shopping options for the community. This will be the 90th location for Market Basket, which started with a single store in Lowell, Massachusetts, in 1917.
