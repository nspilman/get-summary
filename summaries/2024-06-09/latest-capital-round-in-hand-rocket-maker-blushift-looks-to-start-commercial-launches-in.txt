**Summary of the Article:**

BluShift Aerospace, a startup based at Brunswick Landing, has secured $2.3 million to boost the development of small satellite launches using its proprietary biobased fuel. The funds aim to accelerate efforts for commercial suborbital launches by 2025. The financing includes $1.3 million from a seed round led by Late Stage Capital and a $1 million match from a federal Small Business Innovation Research grant. The company, founded in 2014, is working on eco-friendly rockets and plans to conduct a full-duration “static-fire” engine test soon. BluShift aims to serve universities, corporations, and federal agencies, focusing on rapid and affordable small satellite deployment.
