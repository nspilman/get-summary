The Augusta Downtown Alliance has appointed Jeremy Ashlock as its new executive director, succeeding Michael Hall who resigned last fall. Ashlock, who has been on the board since late 2021, will focus on community organizing and stakeholder engagement to enhance the vitality and cultural vibrancy of downtown Augusta. He brings retail experience from owning a juice and smoothie bar in Hallowell and has consulted for the Maine Space Grant Consortium. Ashlock holds a bachelor's degree in financial economics and an MBA from the University of Maine.
