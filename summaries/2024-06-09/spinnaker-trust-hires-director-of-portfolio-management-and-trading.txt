Spinnaker Trust, a wealth management firm in Portland, has appointed Scott Stefanowicz as the director of portfolio management and trading. Stefanowicz brings over 20 years of experience, including roles at Key Private Bank and HM Payson. In his new position, he will manage portfolio managers, coordinate with trading partners, and optimize trading software use. Amanda Rand, president and CEO of Spinnaker Trust, praised his strategic insights and leadership qualities. Spinnaker Trust manages over $2.5 billion in assets and offers investment management, trust, estate planning, and tax services.
