Bangor Savings Bank has promoted Ryan Dumond to senior vice president and director of bank fintech partnerships. In this role, Dumond will focus on expanding financial technology partnerships and enhancing digital banking solutions. Dumond, who has a long tenure with the bank, has previously contributed significantly to its Banking-as-a-Service (BaaS) program. His new position aims to strengthen relationships with fintech clients and integrate traditional banking solutions. Bangor Savings Bank is a well-established institution with over 60 branches in Maine and New Hampshire, holding assets of nearly $7.5 billion.
