The city of South Portland has appointed Robert Nadeau as the new director of human resources, succeeding Stephanie Weaver, who retired in May. Nadeau brings extensive experience, having most recently served as vice president of human resources for Twin Rivers Paper and previously held significant roles in other organizations including Cianbro and the Maine Community College System. Nadeau holds a J.D. from New York University School of Law and degrees from the University of Maine. City Manager Scott Morelli praised Nadeau's legal background and people skills, highlighting him as a valuable addition to the city's leadership team. Nadeau will earn an annual salary of $124,664.
