The article discusses Bangor-based engineering firm Haley Ward Inc. appointing five new vice presidents across its departments and service lines. The new vice presidents come from diverse backgrounds, including civil engineering, environmental consulting, natural resource permitting, and land surveying. These appointments are seen as crucial for the company's growth and success. Haley Ward is an employee-owned professional engineering, environmental, and surveying consulting firm with a presence in multiple states, including Maine, New Hampshire, Massachusetts, Connecticut, and Florida.
