The article is about STARC Systems, a manufacturer at Brunswick Landing, planning to build an 85,000-square-foot manufacturing and administrative facility to keep up with the company's growth. STARC Systems produces temporary reusable wall systems for construction containment, aiming to cater to various construction markets. The new facility is being developed by Priority Real Estate Group, and the construction is expected to be finished by the fall of 2025. STARC Systems has experienced significant growth in revenue and employee count, with plans to continue expanding its workforce and product line. The expansion reflects the company's commitment to sustainability and innovation in the construction industry.
