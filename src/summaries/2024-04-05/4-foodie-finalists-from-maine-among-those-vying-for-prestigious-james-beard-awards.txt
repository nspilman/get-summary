Four food establishments from Maine are among the finalists for the prestigious James Beard Restaurant and Chef Awards. These finalists include ZU Bakery for outstanding bakery, Norimoto Bakery for outstanding pastry chef or baker, Woodford Food & Beverage for outstanding hospitality, and Jake Stevens of Leeward for best chef in the Northeast region. Winners will be announced on June 10 at the James Beard Restaurant and Chef Awards ceremony in Chicago. This recognition is part of an annual program by the James Beard Foundation.
