The article discusses the management changes at Freeport Village Station, a shopping center in Freeport, Maine. The property was recently sold at a foreclosure auction for $20 million, and a Boston-based real estate firm, The Wilder Companies, has taken over management and leasing responsibilities. The new manager aims to enhance the shopping experience at the center by appealing to locals, visitors, and tourists. Plans include incorporating Maine culture into the shopping experience and revitalizing the property to attract more foot traffic. The article also highlights the property's location, tenant mix, and upcoming improvements such as creating more gathering spaces and adding experiential elements.
