The article discusses the increase in income needed for homebuyers in Maine following a report by Bankrate. It states that prospective home buyers in Maine now need to earn a minimum of $102,557 annually to afford a house at the state's median sales price. The median home price in Maine has increased to $354,000, causing affordability challenges due to higher prices, interest rates, and a shortage of available properties. The article also highlights that home prices have remained high due to low inventory, while Maine salaries have not kept pace with the rising home prices. First-time homebuyers are facing difficulties due to the disparity between income levels and housing costs.
