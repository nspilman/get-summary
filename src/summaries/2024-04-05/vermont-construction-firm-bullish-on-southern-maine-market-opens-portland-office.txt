A Vermont construction firm, DEW Construction Corp., has expanded into Maine by opening an office in Portland. The firm leased 3,500 square feet of office space in the Old Port neighborhood, reflecting its commitment to growing its services in the Portland area. The firm's president highlighted the significance of Portland as a strategic location for expanding its operations. DEW Construction Corp. has a history of successful projects in Vermont and New Hampshire and is now seeking to establish a presence in Maine by taking on various projects in the region. With a growing number of projects and employees in Maine, the firm decided to open an office in response to the opportunities in the region. The company's revenue growth trends are estimated at around $200 million annually, and it plans to do more hiring in Maine while staying focused on maintaining its company culture.
