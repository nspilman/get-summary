The article discusses the closure of Gneiss Brewing Co., a Limerick brewery that specialized in German-inspired wheat beers, after more than 10 years in business. The decision to close was not sudden, with various challenges such as COVID-related issues, supply chain shortages, and health problems among staff contributing to the closure. The brewery stopped production in November, and only has a few cases and kegs left. The owner plans to sell the business, equipment, building, and land. The article also touches on the challenges faced by Maine's craft beer industry overall, with several breweries closing down in recent months.
