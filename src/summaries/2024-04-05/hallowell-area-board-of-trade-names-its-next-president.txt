The article discusses the appointment of Nick Lacasse as the president of the Hallowell Area Board of Trade. Lacasse has a background in business management and a commitment to civic engagement. He is also a founding partner of an energy development and consulting firm. Lacasse's experience includes roles at Novo Energy Sales and Renewable Energy. He holds a bachelor's degree in economics from the College of Holy Cross in Worcester, Mass. Lacasse expressed enthusiasm for collaborating with board members and stakeholders to contribute to the growth and innovation of the Hallowell community.
