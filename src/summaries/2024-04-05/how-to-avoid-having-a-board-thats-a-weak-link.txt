The article discusses strategies for strengthening a company's board of directors in terms of cybersecurity. It emphasizes the importance of having board members with cyber training and suggests actions like providing cybersecurity education, having parallel systems in place, and engaging white-hat hackers to enhance board cybersecurity understanding. The article underlines the potential vulnerabilities and risks businesses face due to cyber threats and provides practical suggestions to mitigate these risks by enhancing the cybersecurity knowledge and preparedness of the board members.
