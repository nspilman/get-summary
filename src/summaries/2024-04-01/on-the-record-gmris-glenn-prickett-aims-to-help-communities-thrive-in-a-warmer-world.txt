Glenn Prickett, the president and CEO of the Gulf of Maine Research Institute, discusses his interest in environmental and climate issues rooted in his upbringing in nature. He explains the importance of their mission in addressing climate change, healthy oceans, sustainable seafood, and the blue economy. The institute aims to help coastal communities adapt to the impacts of climate change, including sea level rise and stronger storms. They focus on attracting young people to the blue economy workforce, promoting sustainable seafood, and managing funding challenges.
