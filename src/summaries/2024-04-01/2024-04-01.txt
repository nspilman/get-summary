The article discusses the proposed Gorham Connector by the Maine Turnpike Authority, aiming to link Gorham with the Maine Turnpike. This project aims to ease traffic congestion along local streets and back roads due to population growth and new businesses. However, some residents have concerns about its impact, especially on Smiling Hill, a family farm in Westbrook. The article explores the implications of this proposed highway spur and its broader impact on southern Maine.
