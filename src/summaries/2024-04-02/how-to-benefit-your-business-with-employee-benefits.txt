The article discusses the importance of employee benefits for small businesses in Maine. It highlights the impact of a well-rounded benefits package on employee satisfaction, financial security, and loyalty. The piece emphasizes that offering support such as disability insurance, accident coverage, and mental health resources can enhance the overall employee experience and lead to increased employee retention. The author, Chris Pyne from Unum, stresses that investing in comprehensive employee benefits is crucial in today's labor market to meet employee expectations and demonstrate care and support for employees.
