Maine Savings Federal Credit Union has added Kaitlin Ferland and Kash Keezer to its mortgage loan officers team. Kaitlin Ferland, with over a decade of experience in real estate lending, has joined Maine Savings to provide lending support and financial education to members. Kash Keezer, with nearly a decade of experience in financial services and mortgage lending, aims to offer tailored mortgage experiences to meet the unique needs of members. Maine Savings FCU, established in 1961, serves over 35,000 members and has branches in various locations. It was ranked No. 2 among the largest Maine-based credit unions in the 2024 Mainebiz Book of Lists.
