The article discusses Mainebiz winning four awards from the New England Newspaper and Press Association's Better Newspaper Competition. The awards were for Matt Selva, the Art Director, who won three awards for his illustration work on a cover story about the banking sector. Senior Writer Renee Cordes also won an award for her cover story about female farmers in Maine. The awards highlight the hard work and creativity of the Mainebiz team. Additionally, the Worcester Business Journal, a sister publication, won eight NENPA awards as well.
