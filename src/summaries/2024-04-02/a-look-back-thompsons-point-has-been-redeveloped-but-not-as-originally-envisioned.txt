The article discusses the redevelopment of Thompson's Point in Maine. Initially proposed as the Atlantic Portland project in 1995, including plans for an aquarium, convention center, and transportation hub, the project did not materialize. However, today Thompson's Point features various attractions like Bissell Bros. Brewery, office space, a skating rink, and event space instead. The Children’s Museum & Theatre of Maine relocated to a new 30,000-square-foot facility at Thompson’s Point after a successful capital campaign.
