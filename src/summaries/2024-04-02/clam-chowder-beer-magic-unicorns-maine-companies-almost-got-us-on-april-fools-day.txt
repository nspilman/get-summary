The article discusses the various fun and creative April Fools' Day pranks pulled by Maine companies. Shipyard Brewing teased a Clam Chowdah IPA, while Cushnoc Brewing joked about using Kennebec River floodwater for their lager. Other pranks included a fake tram connecting ski resorts, magical unicorns at an agriculture center, river cruises, and a faux Hooters restaurant opening in Portland's Old Port.
