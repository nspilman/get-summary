The article provides advice on what funders look for in a business plan. Funders, including bankers, grantors, and investors, want to see a comprehensive business plan that demonstrates the investment's potential for generating a return. The plan should showcase execution capability, market understanding, growth potential, and market potential. It should include details such as revenue forecasts, expense estimates, cash flow projections, market research, industry benchmarks, and repayment schedules. Additionally, the article emphasizes the importance of having a capable and experienced management team and seeking advice from experienced mentors.
